How to Resume a Conversation

Pick up right where you left off in any previous session.

  1. Go to Conversations or Sessions to browse your chat history.
  2. Find your session by topic, date, or keyword search. Click a session in Sessions to open it directly in the Conversations viewer.
  3. Click the "Resume" button — this copies the resume command to your clipboard: cd "PROJECT_DIR"; claude --resume SESSION_ID
  4. Paste the command in your terminal (PowerShell or bash) and press Enter.
Quick resume options:
• claude --continue — instantly resume the most recent session (any directory)
• claude --resume — opens an interactive picker to choose from recent sessions
• claude --resume SESSION_ID — resume a specific session (must be in the correct project directory)
